## Preconditions

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Delete a course category on Dynamic Field Settings page.
2. In the search bar, search for the deleted category's name.
3. Reload the page.
4. Navigate to the Course Category section/tab.
5. Observe the 'Add Course' form rendering.
---
## Expected Result
The 'Add Course' form should render normally within its designated layout/section without overlaying other page content, regardless of prior search/delete/reload sequence.
 
---
## Actual Result
The 'Add Course' form appears as an unintended overlay on top of the page content after this specific navigation sequence.
