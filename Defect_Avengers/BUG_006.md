## Preconditions
- An existing service record is available to edit.
---
## Steps to Reproduce
1. Navigate to Dynamic Field Settings page.
2. Click Edit on any service record.
3. Enter `=$` (or other special characters) in the Service Name field.
4. Click 'Update Service'.
---
## Expected Result
The field should validate input and reject/sanitize special characters or formula-prefix characters (`=`, `+`, `-`, `@`), showing an appropriate validation error.
 
---
## Actual Result
The invalid value `=$` is accepted and saved successfully with no validation error shown.

## Impact
This is a data-integrity risk and, since the value may later be exported to Excel/CSV, also poses a formula-injection risk (CSV/Excel injection) to any downstream consumer opening the export.
